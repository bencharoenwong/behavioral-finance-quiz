#### Coins (Quick Rewards)
- Earn 10-50 coins per correct answer

- Earn 1 gem per module completion

## ðŸ’¡ Key Insight

The current app is a **one-and-done experience**. To create true engagement:

1. **Daily habit** > One-time completion
2. **Ongoing progression** > Fixed endpoint
3. **Social connection** > Solo experience
4. **Practical application** > Pure learning
5. **Frequent small wins** > Rare big achievements

Transform from **"Take the quiz"** to **"Train your investor brain daily"**.
